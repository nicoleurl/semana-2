Artist of the month: Maria Svarbova

Photographer Maria Svarbova captures synchronistic images in Soviet era swimming pools.

The series, In the Swimming Pool, began in 2014 and is her largest to date. Recently she published a book on the project through The New Heroes and Pioneers aptly titled The Swimming Pool Book which you can pre-order on Amazon.
